The University of Georgia Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Education (UGA SNAP-Ed) welcomes the community's comments. Community-contributed content on recipes and elsewhere represent the opinion of the specific author and do not represent UGA SNAP-Ed. UGA SNAP-Ed asks that users do not post unauthorized commercial solicitations (such as spam); bully, intimidate, or harass any user; post content that is hateful, threatening, pornographic, or that contains nudity or graphic or gratuitous violence; or do anything unlawful, misleading, malicious, or discriminatory. UGA SNAP-Ed reserves the right, but is not obligated, to remove comments that contain commercial solicitations; are factually erroneous/libelous; are wildly off-topic; that cannot be translated into English by Google Translate or other free online translation software; or that otherwise violate these statements; or at the discretion of this page's administrators. If you find abusive content, we encourage you to report it by emailing foodtalk[at]uga[dot]edu. UGA SNAP-Ed thanks you in advance for your contributions and for your help in creating a safe and vibrant online community.
